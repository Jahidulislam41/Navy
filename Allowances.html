FACILITIES AND PAY & ALLOWANCES






Facilities :

* Free medical service and health care for self, family, parents & servants.
* Free furnished accommodation with nominal charges for gas, water & electricity.
* Ration for self, family & servant with subsidized rate during service and specified allowance on retirement as prescribed by the government from time to time.
* Professional clothing / gears with subsidized price.
* Transportation facility both free and on payment.
* Secured and disciplined life with good societal environment.
* Children education facilities.
* Priority for children in admission into Cadet Colleges, Armed Forces Medical College & MIST.
* Subsidized rate for hiring different military auditoriums for personal use.
* Health club, swimming pool & different sports facilities at BAF Messes.
* Park and entertainment facility for the children.
* Opportunity to serve in UN missions.
* Group and Flying Risk Insurance for officers of different branches as applicable.
* A Plot in Defense Officers Housing society (DOHS) for officers.
* Opportunities of re-employment in different Govt or Non-government Organizations.
* Medical facilities for self and family even after retirement.

Pay & Allowances: BAF Officers :


You can earn a salary in the Air Force that compares very favorably to rates in the civilian world. There are endless opportunities to improve your base salary with the many financial incentives available to you. The longer you stay with us, the greater your potential is to be earning great pay.
